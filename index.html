The Story

About CalmCampus

Why it exists, how it works, and who built it.

🌱 Why We Built This

Student mental health is at a crisis point. Exam pressure, social anxiety, burnout affect millions daily — yet most never seek help due to stigma, cost, or lack of access. CalmCampus fills that gap: free, anonymous, always available.

⚙️ How It Works

CalmCampus combines clinically-inspired tools (breathing, mood journaling, habit science) with an LLM (via Groq) for personalized, intelligent support. Everything runs in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

📅 Journey

SIP 2026 — NeuroWatch

Started as an AI mental health monitoring system at Woxsen University. Palash built the full prototype solo.

Research & Design

Studied CBT, breathing science, and student mental health reports to design tools that actually help.

CalmCampus v3 — Live

Independently evolved into a full AI-powered wellness platform with gamification, anonymous auth, and counselling.

🛡️ Privacy Promise

We take anonymity seriously:

✅ No real identity collected
✅ No analytics or tracking
✅ No data sold or shared
✅ No university integration
✅ AI chats not stored
✅ Most features work offline

👨‍💻 Developed by Palash Mulchandani

CalmCampus is a solo portfolio project — pure HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no shortcuts, just craft. Powered by Llama (via Groq) for counselling and study planning, served through a secure serverless proxy. Built at Woxsen University to solve a real student problem experienced firsthand.
